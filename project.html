Pizza is more than just food; it’s a way of life. As they say, “You can’t make everyone happy; you’re not pizza.” A slice a day might not officially keep the doctor away, but it surely keeps the sad away. In a world full of challenges, pizza is the only love triangle anyone would want to be part of. After all, “In pizza we crust.” There’s something undeniably comforting about it—“Life happens, but pizza helps,” and when it comes to bringing people together, “Home is where the pizza is.”


Sometimes, it feels like pizza is the answer, no matter the question. Happiness might be hard to buy, but “Money can buy pizza, and that’s kind of the same thing.” In times of celebration, stress, or just any random Tuesday, pizza is there, reminding us that “Any time is a good time for pizza.” So next time life gets overwhelming, remember: “All you need is pizza and a little bit of cheese.”
